Personal Data Platform

Secure AI-driven personal data management solution

The Data Privacy Crisis

Your data isn't yours anymore. Tech giants collect, store, and monetize personal information with little transparency or user control. Data breaches expose millions of records annually, while users have no idea what data is collected, how it's used, or who has access to it. The current system prioritizes corporate profits over individual privacy.

Impact: Privacy violations, identity theft, manipulation through targeted content, and loss of personal autonomy

Our Vision for Data Ownership

Personal Data Platform is our research concept for giving individuals complete control over their personal information. We're exploring how AI could help users understand privacy implications, manage data sharing permissions, and even monetize their own data if they chooseâ€”all while maintaining security and privacy.

Transparency Note: This is currently in the concept research phase. We're studying blockchain technologies, privacy frameworks, and exploring partnerships with privacy-focused organizations.

The Challenge

Today's digital landscape presents a paradox: users need to share personal data to access services, yet face increasing privacy risks and lack control over how their information is used.

Our Solution

A user-centric data ecosystem where information is stored securely, with AI algorithms helping users understand privacy implications and control exactly what is shared with which services.
