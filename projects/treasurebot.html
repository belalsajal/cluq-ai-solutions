TreasureBot

AI-powered product discovery bot

Overview

An intelligent product discovery system using advanced AI to help users find exactly what they're looking for, even with imprecise queries, transforming the e-commerce search experience.

The Challenge

Traditional e-commerce search often fails when users don't know exact terminology for products or have complex requirements, leading to frustrated customers and missed sales.

Our Solution

TreasureBot bridges this gap by understanding natural language, inferring intent, and connecting users with products that match their needs, even if search terms don't directly match product descriptions.

Key Features

Natural language search
Visual search capabilities
Personalized recommendations
Cross-category matching
Sentiment analysis

Impact

53% higher satisfaction
22% better conversion
