The Critical Problem We're Addressing

Urban waste management is fundamentally broken. Current systems rely heavily on manual sorting, leading to contamination rates of 30-40% in recycling streams. This inefficiency costs cities millions annually while contributing to environmental degradation.

Waste workers face significant health risks from handling contaminated materials, communities struggle with overflowing bins and inefficient collection routes, and the environment suffers from improper waste processing.
