Maximize CV

AI-powered resume optimization tool

Overview

An intelligent resume optimization platform that helps job seekers create tailored, ATS-friendly resumes that highlight relevant qualifications for specific job postings, increasing chances of landing interviews.

The Challenge

Over 75% of submitted resumes are filtered out by applicant tracking systems before reaching human recruiters, reducing qualified candidates' chances of consideration.

Our Solution

Maximize CV analyzes job descriptions and compares them with user resumes, identifying missing keywords and providing specific optimization recommendations for each application.

Key Features

AI-powered keyword optimization
Real-time resume scoring
Industry-specific templates
ATS simulation testing
Skills gap analysis

Personalized career guidance
