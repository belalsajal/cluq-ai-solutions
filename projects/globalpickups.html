Concept Research Phase

Revolutionary Logistics Concept

Global Pickups represents our vision for transforming international shipping through community-powered peer-to-peer delivery. We're currently researching legal frameworks, safety protocols, and building partnerships with travel communities to make this sustainable logistics solution a reality.

The Global Shipping Crisis

International shipping is fundamentally broken. Cross-border delivery costs are astronomical (often 50-100% of item value), delivery times are unpredictable, and traditional shipping generates massive carbon emissions. Meanwhile, millions of travelers have unused luggage space and could benefit from earning travel money.

Community-Driven Solution

Global Pickups creates a secure network where travelers monetize unused luggage space by delivering items along planned routes. Our AI matching system analyzes travel patterns and item specifications to create optimal delivery partnerships while building global community connections.
