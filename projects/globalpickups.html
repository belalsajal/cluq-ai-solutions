Global Pickups

Peer-to-peer delivery platform

Overview

A community-based delivery platform that connects travelers with people who need items delivered from one location to another, creating an eco-friendly alternative to traditional shipping methods.

The Challenge

Traditional international shipping is expensive, environmentally harmful, and often subject to delays, while thousands of people travel between countries daily with unused luggage space.

Our Solution

Global Pickups creates a secure network where travelers monetize unused luggage space by delivering items along planned routes, with AI matching analyzing travel patterns and item specifications.

Key Features

AI-powered matching system
Real-time tracking
Trust and reputation scoring
Secure payment processing
Carbon footprint tracking

Impact

10,000+ successful deliveries
60% lower delivery costs
75 tons CO2 emissions saved
Income for frequent travelers
