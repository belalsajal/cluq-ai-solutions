Maximize CV

AI-powered resume optimization tool

The Job Application Problem

Job applications are a black hole. 75% of resumes never reach human eyes due to ATS (Applicant Tracking System) filters. Qualified candidates are rejected for missing keywords, wrong formatting, or failing to match the system's expectations. The process is opaque, frustrating, and wastes everyone's time.

Impact: Qualified candidates get ignored, companies miss great talent, and job seekers waste time on ineffective applications

Our Solution Approach

Maximize CV is our concept for an AI-powered resume optimization tool that could help job seekers understand and navigate ATS systems. By analyzing job descriptions and providing specific recommendations, we aim to level the playing field and help qualified candidates get noticed.

Transparency Note: This is currently in the development phase. We're building prototypes and conducting user research to validate the effectiveness of our optimization algorithms.

The Challenge

Over 75% of submitted resumes are filtered out by applicant tracking systems before reaching human recruiters, reducing qualified candidates' chances of consideration.

Our Solution

Maximize CV analyzes job descriptions and compares them with user resumes, identifying missing keywords and providing specific optimization recommendations for each application.
