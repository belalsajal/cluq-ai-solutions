Our Services

What AI services does CLUQ AI offer?

We offer comprehensive AI solutions including machine learning model development, natural language processing, computer vision, predictive analytics, AI strategy consulting, and custom AI application development. Our services span from initial consultation to full implementation and ongoing support.

Do you work with small businesses or only enterprises?

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to large enterprises. Our scalable solutions and flexible pricing models ensure that organizations at any stage can benefit from AI technology. We tailor our approach to match your specific needs and budget.

Can you integrate AI into existing business systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in seamless integration of AI solutions into existing business infrastructure. Our team ensures minimal disruption to your current operations while maximizing the benefits of AI implementation.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have experience across multiple industries including finance, healthcare, e-commerce, manufacturing, logistics, and technology. Our adaptable AI solutions can be customized for any industry's specific requirements and compliance needs.

Process & Timeline

How long does a typical AI project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple AI implementations can take 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive enterprise solutions may require 3-6 months. We provide detailed timeline estimates during our initial consultation phase.

What does your development process look like?

Our process includes: 1) Discovery and requirements analysis, 2) Data assessment and preparation, 3) AI model development and training, 4) Testing and validation, 5) Integration and deployment, 6) Training and support. We maintain close communication throughout each phase.

Do you provide project updates and milestones?

Yes, we believe in transparent communication. You'll receive regular progress updates, milestone reports, and have access to our project management platform where you can track development progress in real-time.

Pricing & Payment

How do you structure your pricing?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, hourly consulting rates, and retainer agreements. Pricing depends on project complexity, timeline, and required resources. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs.

What payment terms do you offer?

We typically work with milestone-based payments for larger projects and monthly billing for ongoing services. Payment terms are flexible and can be customized based on your organization's requirements and cash flow needs.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we provide a free initial consultation to understand your needs, assess your current systems, and recommend the best AI solutions for your business. This helps us create an accurate project proposal and timeline.

Support & Maintenance

What kind of support do you provide after deployment?

We offer comprehensive post-deployment support including system monitoring, performance optimization, bug fixes, feature updates, and user training. Our support packages are designed to ensure your AI solutions continue performing optimally.

How do you handle AI model updates and improvements?

We continuously monitor model performance and provide regular updates to improve accuracy and efficiency. Our maintenance agreements include periodic model retraining with new data to ensure optimal performance as your business evolves.

Do you provide training for our team?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive training programs for your team, including system usage, best practices, troubleshooting, and AI literacy. We ensure your team is confident and capable of maximizing the benefits of your AI implementation.
