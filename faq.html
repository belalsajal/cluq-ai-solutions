Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI solutions and services

What industries do you serve?

We provide AI solutions for a wide range of industries including healthcare, finance, manufacturing, retail, logistics, and more. Our technologies are adaptable to most business contexts and can be customized to your specific industry needs.

How much does an AI solution cost?

The cost of an AI solution varies depending on the complexity, scale, and specific requirements of your project. We offer different pricing models including fixed project fees, subscription-based services, and outcome-based pricing. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do I need a large amount of data to use AI?

While having more data typically improves AI performance, we can work with various data scenarios. For organizations with limited data, we can employ techniques like transfer learning, data augmentation, or synthetic data generation to build effective solutions.

How long does it take to implement an AI solution?

Implementation timelines vary based on project complexity and scope. Simple projects might take a few weeks, while more complex enterprise-wide systems could take several months. We follow an agile approach, often delivering value in incremental phases rather than waiting for complete implementation.

How do you ensure data privacy and security?

We take data security very seriously. Our solutions comply with relevant regulations like GDPR and CCPA. We implement encryption, secure access protocols, and can deploy solutions on-premises or in private clouds if needed. We sign comprehensive data processing agreements and can work with your security team to address any specific concerns.

Do you offer support after implementation?

Yes, we provide ongoing support and maintenance for all our solutions. We offer different support tiers ranging from basic email support to 24/7 dedicated support. We can also train your team to manage the system internally and provide knowledge transfer as needed.

Can your solutions integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, our solutions are designed with interoperability in mind. We can integrate with most common enterprise systems, databases, and APIs. During the initial assessment phase, we'll evaluate your current technology stack and design the solution to work seamlessly with your existing infrastructure.
